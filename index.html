Bionic Reading Assistant

Bionic Reading is a method that aims to improve reading efficiency by emphasizing the first part of each word. This is based on the idea that our brains recognize words by their first few letters, and that by making the first half of each word bolder, the brain can process the information more quickly. Although it is a relatively new concept, some users have reported positive experiences with Bionic Reading in terms of speed and comprehension.
